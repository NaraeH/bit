제품관리

목록조회:
ex1) http://localhost:8080/prodcut/list
ex2) http://localhost:8080/prodcut/list?pageNo=페이지번호
ex3) http://localhost:8080/prodcut/list?pageNo=페이지번호&pageSize=페이지당출력개수

제품추가:Create
ex1) http://localhost:8080/prodcut/add?name=제품명&qty=수량&mkno=제조사번호

제품조회:Retrieve or Read
ex1) http://localhost:8080/prodcut/view?no=제품번호

제품변경:Update
ex1) http://localhost:8080/prodcut/add?no=제품번호&name=제품명&qty=수량&mkno=제조사번호

제품삭제:Delete
ex1) http://localhost:8080/prodcut/delete?no=제품번호
